Morale of the story

  • External information is dangerous
  • It’s not the number of purchases that determine identifiability. It’s the number of purchasers
  • Aircloak does per user anonymization

Low count filtering

In summary

Information shared by enough distinct users is generally safe. It gets through the filter.

“Enough” means ~5 (more later)
